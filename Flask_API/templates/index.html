Plant predictor

Welcome to our plant harvesting prediction AI application! This application is designed to help farmers and gardening enthusiasts predict when their plants are ready to be harvested. By inputting a regular image and a depth image, the AI will provide an estimate of when the plant will be ready for harvest. This can help optimize crop yields and reduce waste.

The AI has been specifically trained for lettuce. To get started, simply click on the button below. Upload images of your plants and let our system do the rest. Happy harvesting!
